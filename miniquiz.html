Have You Accidentally Joined a Cult?

1. Are you lonely and in desperate need of friends? And/or do you feel isolated from others?

2. Has someone recently invited you to meet their friends and are promoting a new lifestyle change?

3. Have you found yourself selling supplies and promoting content you didn't think you would one year ago?

4. Are there strict rules you have to follow based on activities or appearance (given to you by the people you surround yourself with)?

5. Is questioning authority or critical thinking discouraged among your friends?
